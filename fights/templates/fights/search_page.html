Hello and welcome to the MMA API. There are currently {{ fighters }} fighters and {{ fights }} fights in the database. This page will explain how to access the different endpoints.

To retrieve a list of all fighters or all fights simply go to the /fighter/ or /fight/ endpoint. To find a fighter by name append a querystring to the /fightername/ endpoint and use underscores for spaces. For example /fightername/?name=chris_camozzi. Result are returned most recent first.

Additional fights and filters will be added soon.
